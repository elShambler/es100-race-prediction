Race year

Times are clock time; the race starts 05:00, “+1” marks the second day. Most 2025 check-in times are estimated from recorded departures via a stoppage-time model; the “time in aid stations” chart uses only directly observed check-in/out pairs. Station groups with fewer than 5 runners are not shown.
